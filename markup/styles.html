Our new package of services includes safety culture assessments and subsequent culture transformation processes that are customized to address each organizations’ unique needs.

G2 Integrated Solutions (G2-IS) has announced that it has joined with Mergentis, LLC (Mergentis) in an exclusive alliance agreement to provide Safety Culture Consulting to the Pipeline, Utility and Downstream marketplace. Our new package of services includes, but is not limited to, safety culture assessments and subsequent culture transformation processes that are customized to address each organizations’ unique needs. With API RP 1173 (Pipeline Safety Management Systems, PSMS) on the horizon for pipeline operators, this new G2-IS service capability comes in time to provide the specialist and experienced help to ensure that safety culture is conducive to the successful integration of an organization’s PSMS.

“We are very pleased to be working with the G2-IS team”, said Jade Strong, President & CEO of Mergentis. “With the changes in regulatory requirements approaching the gas utility and pipeline industry, we can share our extensive knowledge and experience working in other high-risk industries who have undergone many of these same changes”. G2-IS’s CEO, David Corbett added, “To be able to offer safety culture consulting services of this caliber, is another example of how G2-IS is positioned to assist our customers with their unique current and future challenges. Our new capability further enhances our mission to be leaders in the provision of holistic solutions to the drive for optimization of pipeline asset performance management and assurance”.

About G2 Integrated Solutions

G2 Integrated Solutions (G2-IS) delivers expertise to pipeline operators, utility companies and other energy stakeholders in seven specialized service disciplines — Asset Integrity, Engineering, Geospatial Systems and Services, Regulatory, Field Services, Technology Services and Strategic Consulting. We provide end-to-end solutions that help manage risk, assure compliance and optimize performance.

Our commitment is to implement innovative, cost-effective solutions that align specifically with our customers’ needs. In order to accomplish this, we have built an organization that is robust in talent and experience. G2-IS has offices located in Houston, Texas (Corporate Headquarters), Angleton, Texas, Concord, California and Denver, Colorado.

For more details on the spectrum of services G2-IS offers, please visit our website at www.g2-is.com.
